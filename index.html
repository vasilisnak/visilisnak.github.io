Vasileios Nakos, Ph.D. | Harvard University

Bio

I am a fourth year ph.D. student in the Theory of Computation group of Harvard University, advised by Prof. Jelani Nelson. My research till now has focused on compressed sensing and streaming algorithms. I am mostly attracted to algorithmic problems that have a flavour of high-dimensional geometry. You can shoot me an e-mail over if you find any paper of mine interesting; I tend to be very responsive.
