Bio

I hold a postdoc position at Saarland University and Max Planck Institute for Informatics, generously funded by Karl Bringmann's ERC starting grant . Before that, I obtained by Ph.D. from Harvard University, advised by Prof. Jelani Nelson. You can contact me at vnakos AT mpi-inf DOT mpg DOT de if you have interest or questions in any of my papers.

Much of my research has focused in sparse recovery: the sample and time efficient recovery of sparse structures via queries from a restricted class. This framework encapsulates compressed sensing, the heavy hitters problem in streaming algorithms, sparse Fourier Transforms and sparse polynomial multiplication, combinatorial group testing, etc. It has also connections with problems such as Subset Sum and with the fields of Additive Combinatorics and pseudorandomness.
